## Overview

The JUICY Academy Metallurgy curriculum teaches the science of metals through Jamaica's metallurgical heritage. Jamaica was the world's largest bauxite producer for decades — the country's geology, economy, and industrial history are inseparable from aluminium metallurgy.

This curriculum starts where Jamaica starts: in the red-earth bauxite deposits of Manchester and Saint Elizabeth, and follows the metal chain from ore to finished product, with engineering science at every step.

## Curriculum Themes

- Crystal structure and the periodic table of metals
- Bauxite mineralogy: gibbsite, boehmite, diaspore
- The Bayer Process: bauxite → alumina
- The Hall-Héroult Process: alumina → aluminium
- Alloy design: strength, ductility, corrosion resistance
- Non-destructive testing and failure analysis
- Recycling metallurgy and the circular economy
